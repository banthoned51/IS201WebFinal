My Worst Three Pokemon



Welcome to my personal rankings of the worst Pokemon from the second generation of the videogame series by from the games. These are my personal opinions, many could and probably do disagree. I will give my reasoning for each of these choices.

  1. Bidoof
    • This pokemon is adorable and lovable. But there are very few that are worse at figthing, and have a worse move set that they learn. There is no express use for this pokemon in actual gameplay outside of having one to teach the HM moves to. Even then, there are cooler pokemon that you could have do that. There is no significance to the gameplay or any aspect of the game that Bidoof provides.
  2. Magikarp
    • Magikarp is quite literally the most useless pokemon, independent of its evolutions. It has no moves besides splash and it cannot learn moves until it evolves. It is also very hard to evolve and it just takes too long. There are viable substitutes for the evolved form so it becomes unnecessary to really have a Magikarp during the game.
  3. Piplup
    • Now I know what you are saying, there are far worse pokemon than Piplup. The thing with Piplup is that he is easily the worst of the starter pokemon that you are given in his generation and he is potentially the worst starter in all of pokemon. Piplup gets so much hype and it does not deserve it. Water is one of the types that gets countered the easiest and it just makes no sense to have a Piplup as your starter or really at any time.
